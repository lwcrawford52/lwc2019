KW Now Weekly News

This weekly email blast was initially a place where agents could find links to recent blog posts, upcoming training, and KW press. I redesigned and built a responsive email template, and worked with the marketing team to refine content and improve engagement.

With regular A/B testing, the newsletter evolved to include free downloads, more curated stories, and personal touches like names and emojis. The newsletter now consistently performs at rates well above the industry standard.
